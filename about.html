Hey, I'm Tuur. I'm Creative, Musical and love working with computers. Therefore, I am studying something in which I can combine all my interests.

From a young age, I have been interested in art, technology and the combination of the two. It all started when my parents bought me a book about art. I was so interested in it that I could name all the paintings and the names of the artists. The interest in art has only grown stronger over the years. I also found computer science and computers very interesting. I thought it was crazy to see how a simple computer just made out of wires could solve such difficult tasks so quickly. that's also why I chose to study Multimedia & Creative technology. It's the perfect combination between art and IT. I hope one day to work as a freelancer or an employee at a creative agency.

As a hobby, I play the viola. I have been doing this since I was 3 years old. I started in a small viola school called Altelier. Then I continued in the music school of Lier. Now, about 15 years later I still play viola. I am very happy that my parents gave me that opportunity to start playing music. it has shaped me into the person I am today.
